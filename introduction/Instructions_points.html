Point Calculation


Your earnings will be calculated in two parts.

The first payoff depends on your and the others' choices. Your choice is the row; the other's choices are the columns. The payoff is the cell in the table corresponding to your and their choice. The total payoff is the sum of all the other players' choices (an example follows).

The second payoff is a bonus ({{bonus}}). You receive it for every player choosing D, if and only if you choose C.
