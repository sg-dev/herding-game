The Game


In this game, you will be playing against 20 players in real-time. You will join an ongoing game. The other players will be waiting until you have completed your round, so please be considerate. In each round, you will be asked to choose one of two actions: C or D. Your choice will be played against all other players simultaneously. You choose one action to play against every player.
