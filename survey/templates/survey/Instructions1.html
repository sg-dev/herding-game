In this experiment you will be randomly paired with another participant or with multiple participants for 1 round: they will be named your "neighbourhood" in the latter case.

Once a match ends, yours and the other participant(s)'s payoffs will be displayed.

You and the participant(s) you are paired with choose simultaneously between two actions, Cooperate and Defect.

When you and the other(s) choose an action, a tax will be applied to your initial payoff and depending on the actions yours and the other(s)'s actions, a portion of yours and the other(s)'s payoff, called bonus, will get redistributed between you and the other(s), unless players choose the same action. In particular, if both cooperate, then they get no bonus while, if both defect, they get subtracted the tax from their original payoff.
