This is a game you are going to play with 20 other randomly chosen players. All players play against each other as illustrated below.




Your task consists in choosing to either collaborate or not with all the other players. This means, that your choice will be played against all other players simultaneously. Your choice and that of the other players’ determines the earnings, as illustrated by the following example. All players are required to pay a share of their earnings into the public pot. However, only collaborating players will receive an equal share of the public pot.

In each round you choose one action which is then played agains every single player. The total earning for the round is the sum of the earning of the interactions. Moreover, you will be placed in an ongoing game with other players and you will be able to see their choices in the last round.

Taxation

The earnings of all players are taxed and the taxed amount is placed in the public pot. The public pot is then redistributed equally among the collaborating players. Suppose for simplicity, that you only play with one player. You choose not to collaborate, while Player X collaborates. Consider the example where the tax rate is 50% and the earnings are the ones shown in the table above.

  1. You earn 8 and Player X earns 5 points.
  2. Given the tax rate of 50% you pay 4 and Player X pays 2.5 into the public pot. The public pot now contains 6.5.
  3. The 6.5 are distributed equally among the players choosing to collaborate. In this case only Player X collborates and gets 6.5 while you get 0. In total you receive 4 (4 + 0) and player X receives 9 (2.5 + 6.5).
Consider a second example with a tax rate of 10%.
  1. You earn 8 and Player X earns 5 points.
  2. Given the tax rate of 10% you pay 0.8 and Player X pays 0.5 into the public pot. The public pot now contains 1.3.
  3. The 1.3 are distributed equally among the players choosing to collaborate. In this case only Player X collborates and gets 1.3 while you get 0. In total you receive 7.2 (7.2 + 0) and player X receives 5.8 (4.5 + 1.3).


To recap, the game consists of 3 phases:

  1. Game Phase You choose either to collaborate or not and get an initial earning.
  2. Tax Phase Tax is collected from all players’ and placed in a public pot.
  3. Redistribution Phase This public pot is redistributed equally among collaborating players.
